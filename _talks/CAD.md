IvLabs, in association with the IEEE VNIT Student Chapter, organized a comprehensive workshop on Computer-Aided Design (CAD). The session saw an enthusiastic participation of 185 undergraduate and graduate students.

The workshop covered a wide range of topics, starting from the fundamentals of orthographic projection to hands-on implementation using SolidWorks, providing participants with both theoretical knowledge and practical experience.
